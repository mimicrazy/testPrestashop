Package: Swift\Message

Mime is the underbelly for Messages, Attachments, Parts, Embedded Images, Forwarded Mail, etc In fact, every single component of the composed email is simply a new Mime document nested inside another When you piece an email together in this way you see just how straight-forward it really is
